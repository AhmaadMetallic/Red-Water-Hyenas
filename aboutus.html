About Flu Fighters
Flu fighters is an app to assist with reducing the time from start of flu season to vaccination. By leveraging available APIs from pharmacies and medical providers, we reduce the time between threat level awareness and vaccination. Research continues by institutions such as the CDC, Harvard University, and Google to establish a reliable flu forecasting model. As one does not exist today and the field is highly complex, we have opted for a novel way to focus on raising awareness and prevention in communities. This project was the result of the collaborative efforts of Nitesh Bhatia, Jimin Yeom, Jermain Foster, Priscila Deavila and Trevor Sheffield, in accordance with the requirements of the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp, powered by Trilogy Education Services, through Georgia Tech Professional Education at Georgia Institute of Technology.
